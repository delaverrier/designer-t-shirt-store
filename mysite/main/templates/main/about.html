The color white symbolizes purity, elegance, and perfection.

In GU design, it is used not only as the primary color but also in combination with other shades, creating unique combinations.

He notes that GU's audacity and courage made him see the world in a different light.

GU inspires and expands horizons in the fashion industry, allowing designers to explore new frontiers and express their creative essence.
